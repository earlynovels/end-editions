About

END Epistolary Editions presents a selection of unique or rare eighteenth-century epistolary novels held by Penn Libraries' Kislak Center for Special Collections. The texts have been prepared by Alice McGrath and other members of the Early Novels Database team from digital surrogates created by the Schoenberg Center for Electronic Text and Image. While these editions are designed for reading and annotation, you can download our corpus of 75 novels here. These editions and this site are built using Ed, a jekyll theme for static digital editions in line with principles of minimal computing.
